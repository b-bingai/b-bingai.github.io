The bing ia provides a better quality interface, a new Bing chat experience, and relevant answers to the complex query. Through this program, you can create images by textual description. How to use the Bing AI?


Follow the following steps for using Bing AI:
  1. Turn on your device and open Microsoft Edge Web Browser.
  2. Visit the bing ia official website.
  3. Now, you can sign into your Microsoft account by entering the username and password.
  4. Click on the Join and create the waitlist option.
  5. Once you've joined the list then you'll get an email "You're in"!
  6. You can ask your complex queries and get relevant answers.
  7. Finally, you can use the Bing AI new feature.
